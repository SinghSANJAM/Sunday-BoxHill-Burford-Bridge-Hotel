Culinary Excellence

Delight in Every Bite

Nestled in Surrey’s North Downs, Sunday Box Hill Burford Bridge Hotel offers unforgettable dining experiences. Enjoy fine dining at Emlyn Restaurant or bespoke private dining events, with seasonal menus showcasing the best of British cuisine.

Emlyn Restaurant

Emlyn Restaurant, our signature fine dining venue, offers an elegant setting with seasonal menus featuring locally sourced ingredients, perfect for special occasions. Spanning 15m x 10m x 3m, located on the ground floor, it accommodates up to 80 guests in a refined ambiance. The Lounge and Garden Terrace are also available for dining.
