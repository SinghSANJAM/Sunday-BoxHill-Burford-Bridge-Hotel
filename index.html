A Legacy of Charm

Nestled at the foot of Surrey’s picturesque Box Hill, our 4-star hotel blends centuries of history with modern comfort. Once hosting Lord Nelson, Jane Austen, and John Keats, it offers elegant rooms, fine dining at Emlyn Restaurant, and serene gardens along the River Mole.

Choose from Classic Rooms for a cozy base, Superior Rooms with balconies and sofa beds overlooking the grounds, or Privilege Rooms with historic charm and extra amenities, perfect for exploring Surrey Hills or nearby Reigate, Leatherhead, and Dorking.

Getting Here

Arrive Easily

Nearest stations: West Humble – 700 yards / Dorking – 1.1 miles / Deepdene – 1.1 miles

Bus 465 connects to Dorking and Kingston, stopping near the hotel.

Complimentary parking on-site, just off the A24 and B2209, 15 miles from Gatwick Airport.

From M25:

Exit at Junction 9 (Leatherhead).

Follow A24 south towards Dorking.

Continue on A24 for approximately 3 miles.

The hotel is on the left, at the foot of Box Hill.

12.4 mi to Gatwick airport.

Pet-Friendly

Welcome Four-Legged Guests

Pets are welcome in Superior Rooms (max two pets under 15kg, £20/night per pet, service dogs free). Enjoy treats, water bowls, and toy balls by the garden door. Pets must be on leads in public areas (bar and restaurant, except breakfast) and supervised. A £250 cleaning fee may apply for damages.
