Meetings & Events

Inspire Your Team

Host impactful events at Sunday Box Hill Burford Bridge Hotel, nestled at the foot of Surrey’s North Downs. With 5 versatile meeting spaces, including the historic Tithe Barn, our venues are perfect for conferences, board meetings, or team-building activities in our 3 acres of landscaped gardens. Our dedicated meeting planner ensures a seamless experience.
