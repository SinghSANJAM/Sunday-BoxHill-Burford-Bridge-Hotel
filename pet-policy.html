Pet Policy

Bring Your Pets to Box Hill

Welcome to Sunday Box Hill Burford Bridge Hotel

We are excited to extend a warm welcome to you and your four-legged travellers. Please review the helpful information for pet owners below.

Allowed Pets

  • Pets are allowed in Superior Rooms. (Check with our reservation team for other rooms.)
  • A maximum of two pets per room; Pets must weigh less than 15kg unless hotel management gives prior approval.
  • A £20 per night fee per pet applies. If two small dogs (under 3kg each) stay together, only one charge applies.
  • Service dogs are free of charge.
  • Pets are welcome in the bar, restaurant (with the exception of breakfast), and public areas – They must be on leads at all times.
  • You will be able to find water, water bowls and treats next to the garden door.
  • Pets cannot be left alone in room. Housekeeping will only clean rooms when pets are not present.

Guest Responsibilities, Damages and Cleaning

  • Pets must be supervised and on leads at all times.
  • Owner understands that the pet should not disrupt the quiet enjoyment of other guests due to excessive barking.
  • Owners must clean up after pets and dispose of waste properly (no dog waste bins in the garden).
  • Guests are responsible for any damage caused by pets.
  • A £250 cleaning fee will apply for any extra cleaning or damage after check-out.

Restrictions

  • Only dogs and cats are allowed.
  • Assistance dogs are exempt from charges and restrictions.

Amenities

  • Treats | Water bowl | Toy ball
  • Disposal bags | Bin

By bringing a pet, guests agree to these terms. Thank you for helping us maintain a comfortable environment for everyone.
