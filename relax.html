Serenity Awaits

Your Oasis of Relaxation

Set amidst 3 acres of picturesque gardens at the foot of Surrey’s North Downs, the Lido at Sunday Box Hill Burford Bridge Hotel offers a serene escape. Our heated outdoor pool is perfect for leisurely swims, family fun, or elegant poolside events. Enjoy comfortable loungers, shaded umbrellas, and a tranquil setting ideal for relaxation.

Join the Lido

How to Join

Join our Lido community to enjoy exclusive access to our heated outdoor pool. Memberships can be secured online or in person once our Lido office is operational.

  • Online: Sign up via our membership portal.
  • In Person: Available once the Lido office is up and running.
    Contact us for updates: john-henry@graniteleisure.com or 07904 463 120.
